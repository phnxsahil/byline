Just shipped semantic search on fltrd.tech — and it fundamentally changes how content filtering works.

Instead of rigid keyword matching, filters now understand meaning. A filter for "toxic gaming comments" catches creative misspellings, coded language, and contextual toxicity that rule-based systems miss entirely.

The technical bet: pgvector embeddings with a hybrid retrieval approach (dense + sparse). Latency stayed under 50ms at p99.
